1885. IT WAS RAINING THE NIGHT HE ARRIVED.
COVERED IN BLOOD. SO MUCH BLOOD EVEN THE RAIN COULDN’T WASH IT AWAY.
HOW DID HE GET THAT WAY? WHOSE BLOOD WAS IT?
THE BOY CLAIMED HE DID NOT KNOW, BUT IT WAS CLEAR HE HAD SUFFERED GREATLY IN LIFE.
AN ORPHAN, RAISED ON THE STREETS. HE WAS NEAR DEATH WHEN I FOUND HIM. 
BEING AN ORPHAN MYSELF, I TOOK PITY ON THE LAD. I TOOK HIM IN. AND EVENTUALLY, IN TIME...
