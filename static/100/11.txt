...I TOOK HIM UNDER MY WING.
HE WAS BRIGHT AND LEARNED QUICKLY. MORE THAN ANYTHING, THE BOY WAS FASCINATED BY MY WORK AND WOULD TOUR THE HALLS OF ARKHAM WITH ME.
PERHAPS IT WAS GROWING AMONG THE SUFFERING MASSES OF GOTHAM, BUT HIS FASCINATION SOON BECAME A PASSION.
HE BELIEVED IN THE GOOD OF THIS PLACE. AND IN ME. AND I DARE SAY, WITH NO HEIR OF MY OWN...
...I CAME TO THINK OF HIM AS MY OWN SON. SOMEONE WHO WOULD CARRY ON MY NAME AND MY WORK.
TOMORROW IS A NEW PAGE, FATHER, THE BOY WOULD SAY, NO MATTER HOW BAD THINGS SEEMED AT THE ASYLUM. A PRIVATE PHRASE TO BUOY MY SPIRITS. HE WAS EVER THE OPTIMIST...
...EVEN WHEN HE MET JACK DOE.
JACK WAS BEING PUNISHED FOR BRUTALIZING YET ANOTHER NURSE. HE TAUNTED THE BOY, TRIED TO GET UNDER HIS SKIN, EVER A CRUEL JOKER. BUT STILL...
...THE BOY REMAINED UNDETERRED.
WERE THE LIKES OF JACK BEYOND OUR HELP? I CONFESS, I STARTED TO WONDER...
