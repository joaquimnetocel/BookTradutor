1886. IT WAS ALL DUE TO A DAMNED CARELESS ORDERLY.
I HAD WARNED THE FOOL. TIME AND TIME AGAIN. WHATEVER YOU DO, DO NOT GET TOO CLOSE TO THE INMATES.
HIS KEYS WERE TAKEN. HIS THROAT SLASHED FROM EAR TO EAR WITH A SHARPENED TOOTHBRUSH.
AND HE WAS BUT THE FIRST.
HE RELEASED A NUMBER OF HIS FELLOW INMATES AND THEY MADE THEIR WAY DOWN TO THE FURNACE ROOM...
...DECIDING AGAINST CONFRONTING THE ARMED GUARDS AT THE FRONT DOORS.
WHATEVER THE CASE, THEY QUIETLY WORKED THROUGH THE NIGHT...
...DUG OUT A TUNNEL FROM BEHIND THE FURNACE, AND JUST LIKE THAT...
