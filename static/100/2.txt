These are the writings of Dr. Amadeus Arkham. 
They serve as a chronicle of this infamous place that bears my name...
...Arkham Asylum.
Yet this book is also ther things...
...it is my confession. It holds secrets I will take with me to my grave.
It is also a warning. About the terrible evil I have unleashed upon the world...
...and a plea that you, reader, stop it before it’s too late...
