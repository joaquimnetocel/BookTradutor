OR SO WE THOUGHT.
WE QUICKLY REALIZED THE SOLDIER AT THE DOOR WAS NOT MY FATHER.
THE LETTER HE HANDED MY MOTHER WOULD EXPLAIN EVERYTHING, HE SAID.
AND SO IT DID.
IT CAME FROM THE NEWLY INSTALLED PRESIDENT JOHNSON...
INFORMING US THAT FATHER HAD BEEN KILLED AT THE BATTLE OF PALMITO RANCH. IRONICALLY, A BATTLE FOUGHT AFTER LEE’S SURRENDER. MEANING...
...MY FATHER DIED IN A POINTLESS BATTLE.
IT WAS ALL TOO MUCH FOR MY MOTHER TO TAKE. THE SOLDIER, TO HIS CREDIT, TRIED TO CONSOLE HER.
BUT SHE...
...HAD OTHER IDEAS.
