...BUT THE INCIDENT WOULD SHAPE MY LIFE.
FROM WHEN I FIRST ARRIVED AT THE ELLIOT GOTHAM CITY ORPHANGE AS A SCARED LITTLE BOY...
...TO THE DAY I EMERGED FROM ITS DOORS, A CONFIDENT YOUNG MAN WITH NOTHING MORE THAN THE CLOTHES ON MY BACK AND A DREAM.
A DREAM TO HELP TRAGEDY THAT BEFELL ME PEOPLE LIKE MY MOTHER.
A DREAM TO BUILD A PLACE WHERE PEOPLE SUCH AS HER COULD GET THE CARE THEY NEEDED. WHERE THE BEST SCIENCE OF THE DAY WOULD BE APPLIED TO TREAT THE MIND'S AFFLICTIONS. SO THE TRAGEDY THAT BEFELL ME COULD BE PREVENTED.
A PLACE I WOULD CALL ARKHAM ASYLUM.
WITH MONEY GIVEN TO ME BY THE ELLIOT FAMILY FOUNDATION, I WOULD PURCHASE THE LAND WHERE MY DREAM WOULD COME TO FRUITION.
CHEAP LAND WITH A PAST OF ITS OWN, GRANTED, DUE TO IT BEING WHERE THE BRITISH KEPT COLONIAL PRISONERS OF WAR DURING THE REVOLUTION. WHERE THERE WAS MUCH ABUSE AND LOSS OF LIFE. A PLACE OTHER GOTHAMITES SAID WAS HAUNTED FROM THE START, SOAKED IN BLOOD AND MADNESS.
STILL..NONE OF THAT MATTERED TO ME.
