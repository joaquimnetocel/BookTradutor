IN THE BEGINNING, ARKHAM WAS EVERYTHING I WANTED IT TO BE. A GOOD FACILITY WITH A FULL STAFF OF LIKE-MINDED INDIVIDUALS GETTING PATIENTS THE HELP THEY SO DESPERATELY NEEDED.
EVEN THOSE PATIENTS WHO, WITH GOTHAM BEING GOTHAM, WERE A BIT MORE...
...IN NEED, SHALL WE SAY.
MY MOTHER WOULD BE PROUD, I BELIEVED. THAT SAID...
ARKHAM WAS NOT WITHOUT ITS ISSUES IN THOSE INITIAL YEARS. ONE ISSUE IN PARTICULAR... 
A MAN SO VILE AND HEINOUS I WILL NOT EVEN UTTER HIS NAME. LET'S CALL HIM JACK DOE FOR THE PATIENT I WAS MOST PURPOSE OF THIS JOURNAL.
BY THE TIME HE WAS SENT TO US, JACK HAD MURDERED MULTIPLE PEOPLE, MEN, WOMEN, CHILDREN. AND SOON AFTER HIS ARRIVAL, HE KILLED TWO NURSES AND DISEMBOWELED ANOTHER PRISONER. HE TOOK GLEE IN KILLING. HE WAS ALWAYS LAUGING, A HORRIBLE GRIN ON HIS FACE...
...HE WAS THE TYPE OF INMATE THAT THREATENED THE VERY EXISTENCE OF ARKHAM.
AND SO HE BECAME THE PATIENT. I WAS MOST DETERMINED TO HELP.
