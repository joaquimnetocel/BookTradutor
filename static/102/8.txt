...HE'S CERTAINLY COMMITTED TO THE BIT.
WHEN HE'S NOT THE BAT, HIS ROUTINE IS MONASTICALLY TIGHT.
HE GOES TO WORK, THEN VANISHES INTO THE SHADOWS. THERE'S A RUMOR HE MEETS WITH THE RED HOOD, A SUSPECTED RESISTANCE GROUP.
BUT THE TRUTH IS, HE'S A MAN ALONE. HE EVEN STEERS CLEAR OF HIS MOTHER THESE DAYS.
HE BREAKS FROM ROUTINE ONLY ONCE DURING THESE WEEKS.
TO MAKE A STOP THAT CATCHES ME OFF GUARD.
TO VISIT JOSEPH CHILL. HIS FATHER'S KILLER.
WHAT HE SAYS TO CHILL I DON'T KNOW. I ONLY HAVE VISUAL, NO AUDIO, BUT I CAN IMAGINE.
AND I ONLY LIKE HIM MORE FOR IT.
ANOTHER WEEK IN, MY HANDLER TELLS ME I HAVE TO DROP PROTECTIONS FROM BATMAN. THEY SAY HE'S BECOME TOO VOLATILE A FACTOR.
I CONSIDER PUSHING BACK, TELLING HIM THAT BATMAN HAS HELPED MY MISSION.
THAT IN FACT, HIS PROVOCATIONS HAVE HELPED ME FORM A NEW THEORY OF WHO SIONIS IS, WHAT HE'S A PART OF... I THINK ABOUT MAKING MY CASE.
INSTEAD, I REMOVE THE PROTECTIONS.
SOON AFTER, THE PARTY ANIMALS FIND HIS CAVES IN THE EMPTY HIGH-RISES, AS I DID.
I TRY TO PICTURE BRUCE UP THERE. AT THE MOMENT HE FINALLY UNDERSTANDS.
WHEN HE SEES...
...AND GETS THE MESSAGE. "RETREAT AND REGROUP, BATMAN. R #$@ F R."
