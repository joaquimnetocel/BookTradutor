EIGHTY PERCENT OF ILLEGAL WEAPONS RIDE IT INTO GOTHAM. THE GANGS IN THE ROW GET ALMOST ALL THEIR GUNS AND PILLS ON THE OBS.
THE MATERIAL COMES UP IN PRODUCE TRUCKS BECAUSE WEIGHT RECORDS ARE LESS EXACT. CITRUS, MOSTLY. THEY SAY THE GUNS SMELL LIKE ORANGES WHEN THEY FIRE.
ALL OF IT COMES TO GOTHAM THROUGH CHECKPOINTS RUN BY THE MARONIS AND FALCONES.
THE FAMILIES SHIFT THE HOT POINT EACH MONTH TO AVOID RAIDS...
...RAIDS THEY KNOW ARE COMING ANYWAY. LOOK AT THEM. THEY DO IT ALL IN PLAIN SIGHT. AND WHY NOT? THEY'RE NOT STREET CRIMINALS. THEY HAVE DEALS WITH A DOZEN COMPANIES IN GOTHAM, AND AT LEAST THREE BANKS.
