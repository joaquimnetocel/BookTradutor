PETER PARKER HAS THE PROPORTIONAL SPEED, STRENGTH AND AGILITY OF A SPIDER, ADHESIVE FINGERTIPS AND TOES, AND THE UNIQUE PRECOGNITIVE AWARENESS OF DANGER CALLED “SPIDER-SENSE”! AFTER THE TRAGIC DEATH OF HIS UNCLE BEN, PETER UNDERSTOOD THAT WITH GREAT POWER THERE MUST ALSO COME GREAT RESPONSIBILITY. HE AMAZING SPIDER-MAN!
YEARS AGO, PETER REJECTED A BOND WITH AN AGRESSIVE LIVING ORGANISM CALLED A SYMBIOTE. BUT IT GAINED ACCESS TO PETER’S GENETIC CODE AND JOINED WITH DISGRACED REPORTER EDDIE BROCK, GRANTING HIM AND ITS SUBSEQUENT HOSTS SIMILAR POWERS TO SPIDER-MAN. TOGETHER, THEY WERE VENOM! 
THE VENOM SYMBIOTE AND SPIDER-MAN HAVE MADE THEIR PEACE AFTER YEARS OF CONFLICT, AND MARY JANE WATSON IS NOW BONDED TO THE SYMBIOTE. BUT RECENTLY, BROCK JOINED WITH HIS OLD SYMBIOTE'S PSYCHOTICALLY MURDEROUS OFFSPRING CARNAGE IN HOPE OF KEEPING IT UNDER CONTROL.
THEY MADE A PACT: ONLY SERIAL KILLERS WOULD DIE. BUT THE MOMENT CARNAGE FINDS A POTENTIAL HOST EDDIE CAN'T BEAT, ALL BETS ARE OFF...
